## Notes

Part of the 15-track *My Savior* album. Psalm 124 is a psalm of deliverance and gratitude -- acknowledging that without God's intervention, the speaker would have been overwhelmed. The imagery of escaping "like a bird from the fowler's snare" speaks to liberation and freedom, themes central to Danny's recovery journey.
