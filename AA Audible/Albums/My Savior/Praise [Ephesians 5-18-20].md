# Praise [Ephesians 5:18-20]

Scripture-based spoken word / devotional track from the *My Savior* album.

## Scripture Reference

**Ephesians 5:18-20 (NIV)**

> Do not get drunk on wine, which leads to debauchery. Instead, be filled with the Spirit, speaking to one another with psalms, hymns, and songs from the Spirit. Sing and make music from your heart to the Lord, always giving thanks to God the Father for everything, in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Part of the 15-track *My Savior* album -- scripture-based spoken word and devotional recordings. This passage calls believers to be filled with the Spirit rather than wine, and to express that fullness through music, psalms, and gratitude. A deeply personal scripture given Danny's sobriety journey (sober since 07/12/2009).
