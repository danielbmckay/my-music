# Hey Mama — Kanye West

Kanye wrote this for Donda in 2000, saved it for Late Registration (2005). She cried when she first heard it. Used it as her ringtone. After she died in 2007, it became his most emotional live performance.

Kanye's documentary, story, and career trajectory are a creative inspiration — the self-belief, the production, the willingness to be honest about who you are.
