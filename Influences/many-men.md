# Many Men (Wish Death) — 50 Cent

A song about surviving real threats with faith intact. 50 recorded this after being shot nine times — the danger was literal, not metaphorical.

## Why It Matters

This song captures something real: people who want you gone, and choosing to keep going anyway. Faith and survival in the same breath. That tension lives in the '89 Vision catalog too.

## Connection To The Catalog

The same energy that drives **The Way**, **The Beginning**, and **Satellite** — wounded but still flying, still believing.
