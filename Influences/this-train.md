# This Train — Bob Marley / Bradley Nowell

A gospel standard that both Bob Marley and Bradley Nowell made their own. The fact that Danny's two primary inspirations both gravitated to the same song says everything about the through-line in his music: faith, redemption, and the train that's coming whether you're ready or not.

## Connection To The Catalog

Danny's own "This Train" exists on both the Free Style and Instrumental albums. The song is also referenced in tattoo #24 (Whose World Is This) — "This Train (432hz)."
